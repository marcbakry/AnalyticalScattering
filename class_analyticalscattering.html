Class AnalyticalScattering¶

template<typename T>
class castor::AnalyticalScattering¶

Class for the analytical computation of scattered waves when the incident field is a plane wave propagating along the -e_z axis. The obstacle is assumed to be a non-penetrable sphere with various boundary conditions.

There are three types of computation:

  • field on the surface,

  • field in the propagation domain,

  • farfield amplitude (amplitude of the 0th-order term of the asymptotic expansion at infinity)

Public Functions

matrix<std::complex<T>> sphereHelmholtz(matrix<T> Xobs, T rho, T k, bndc bc, radtyp rt)¶

Computes the exact scattered acoustic field when the obstacle is a sphere. The arguments are:

  • Xobs : observation nodes. If bc == farfield, the Xobs are the directions in which the farfield amplitude will be computed.

  • rho : radius of the sphere.

  • k : wavenumber.

  • rt : type of computation (boundary, domain, infinity)

  • bc : type of boundary condition (Dirichlet, Neumann)

enum castor::radtyp¶

Choose the type of field computation:

  • infinity: compute the far-field amplitude

  • boundary: compute the scattered field on the surface of the scatterer

  • domain: compute the scattered field in the propagation domain
